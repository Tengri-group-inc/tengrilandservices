What we cover

Observation, feasibility & coordination

We perform the non-invasive assessment and hold the calendar. All testing, pumping, and system work is performed by licensed subcontractors we coordinate.

01

Perc & soil feasibility

Preliminary read on percolation and soil suitability, the first answer to "can you build here?" Official percolation testing performed by a certified professional.

02

Water & well sampling

Water samples collected for licensed-lab analysis, bacteria, nitrates, pH, and a feasibility read on existing wells.

03

Septic observation

Non-invasive visual observation of tank, distribution box, and drain field condition. Pumping and inspection coordinated through licensed pros.

04

Drain-line camera

Visual camera observation of existing drain lines, with recording. Any clearing or repair coordinated through licensed subcontractors.

05

Coordinated service & repairs

Filter, riser, and pump work, coordinated end-to-end through licensed professionals. One contact, one schedule.

06

Site access & utilities

Access assessment, clearing coordination, and utility-locate scheduling so the right teams reach the right place.

07

Permit & vendor coordination

Power, road, and permit workflows. We hold the calendar and the paperwork so your team doesn't have to.

08

One accountable partner

A single project manager from first call to final report, scoped in writing, no surprise change orders.
